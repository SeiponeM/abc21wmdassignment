LAST CALL

    Last Call is a 2022 comedy-drama film directed by Paolo Pilladi written by Paolo Pilladi and Greg Lingo based on a story by Greg Lingo, Michael Baughan and Billy Reilly. It was originally under the working title Crabs in a Bucket, The film stars Jeremy Piven , Taryn Manning, Zach McGowan, Jamie Kennedy and Bruce Dern. Release date May 19, 2022 (USA) GenreComedy
