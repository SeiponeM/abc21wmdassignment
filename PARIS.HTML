PARIS

      Paris is a movie based on the Chainsmokers 2017 single hit titled ‘Paris’.
        Drew Taggart and Alex Pall plan on developing a feature based movie with the screenplay rendered by Mickey Rapkin.
          The Chainsmokers produce this feature film via their newly formulated company, Kick The Habit Productions banner.
            The movie is scheduled to release in 2022. Drew and Alex consider this project as highly passionate and inspiring and hold some personal touch to the storyline.
